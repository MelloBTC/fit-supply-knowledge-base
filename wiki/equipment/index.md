# Equipment

Equipment is the highest-priority domain. It should define the taxonomy, specifications, compatibility relationships, condition language, manufacturer knowledge, and model-level facts used across the target-state wiki.

## Focus Areas

- Equipment taxonomy and categories
- Manufacturer and model knowledge
- Specifications and dimensions
- Compatibility and dependencies
- New, used, refurbished, and condition language
- Maintenance, parts, and lifecycle connections
