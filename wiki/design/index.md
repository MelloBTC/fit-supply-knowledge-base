# Design

Design knowledge covers space planning, layouts, 2D/3D workflows, and the decisions that connect customer goals to equipment plans.

## Focus Areas

- Facility types and layout patterns
- Space planning constraints
- 2D and 3D design workflows
- Equipment placement considerations
- Design to sales and project handoffs
- Agent-assisted layout support
